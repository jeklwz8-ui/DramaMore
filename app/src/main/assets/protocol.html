1. Information We Collect

We collect information about you to provide, improve, and optimize the DramaMore App and your user experience. The information we collect falls into two categories: Personal Information and Non-Personal Information. Data privacy and security practices may vary based on your use, region, and age.

1.1 Personal Information

Personal Information is information that can be used to identify, contact, or locate you individually. We collect the following Personal Information only when you choose to provide it or when it is necessary for the App’s functionality:

  • Account Information: When you create an account on DramaMore (e.g., using Google Account, email, or other third-party accounts), we may collect your name, email address, profile picture, and account ID. This helps us authenticate your identity and provide personalized services.
  • Contact Information: If you contact our customer support (e.g., for feedback, complaints, or assistance), we may collect your email address, phone number (if provided), and the content of your communication to respond to your inquiries effectively.
  • Payment Information: If you purchase premium features, subscriptions, or in-app items, we do not directly collect or store your payment details (e.g., credit card numbers, bank account information). All payment transactions are processed by trusted third-party payment processors (e.g., Google Play Billing), and we only receive information about the transaction (e.g., transaction ID, purchase amount, date) to confirm your purchase and provide access to the purchased services.
  • Location Information: With your explicit consent, we may collect your approximate location (e.g., IP address-based location or GPS location) to provide region-specific content recommendations (e.g., dramas popular in your area) and comply with regional content licensing requirements. You can disable location access at any time through your device settings.

1.2 Non-Personal Information

Non-Personal Information is information that cannot be used to identify you individually. We automatically collect this information when you use the App to improve our services and user experience. This includes:

  • Device Information: We collect information about your device, such as device model, operating system (OS) version, device ID, screen resolution, and available storage. This helps us ensure the App is compatible with your device and fix technical issues.
  • App Usage Data: We collect data about how you use the App, including the dramas you watch, the duration of your viewing sessions, the features you use (e.g., favorites, comments, sharing), the time and date of your usage, and your interaction with in-app ads. This data is used to analyze user behavior, optimize content recommendations, and improve the App’s functionality.
  • Log Information: We collect log data, such as your IP address, browser type (if applicable), and the pages or features you access within the App. This helps us monitor the App’s performance, detect unauthorized access, and ensure network security.
  • Anonymous Data: We may aggregate and anonymize Personal Information and Non-Personal Information (e.g., creating statistics about user viewing habits) so that it no longer identifies you. This anonymized data may be used for research, analytics, and improving our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes, in line with the principles of legality, fairness, and necessity:

  • To Provide and Maintain the App: We use your information to operate the App, provide core services (e.g., streaming dramas, account management), and ensure the App runs smoothly and securely. This includes verifying your account, processing your purchases, and delivering the content you request.
  • To Personalize Your Experience: We use your App usage data and preferences to provide personalized content recommendations (e.g., dramas based on your viewing history), customize the App’s interface, and enhance your overall experience.
  • To Improve the App: We analyze the data we collect to identify areas for improvement, develop new features, fix bugs, and optimize the App’s performance and user experience. For example, we may use viewing data to determine which drama genres are popular and add more content in those genres.
  • To Communicate with You: We may use your contact information to send you important updates (e.g., App updates, changes to this Privacy Policy), respond to your inquiries, and send promotional messages (e.g., discounts on premium subscriptions) if you have opted in to receive them. You can unsubscribe from promotional messages at any time.
  • To Ensure Security and Compliance: We use your information to detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and other malicious activities, and to comply with applicable laws, regulations, and Google Play Developer Program Policies.
  • To Display Ads: If you use the free version of the App, we may use your App usage data and non-personal information to display relevant ads. We work with third-party ad providers to deliver ads, and they may collect additional information (as described in Section 3) to personalize ads.

We respect your privacy and will not sell, rent, or share your Personal Information with third parties except as described below. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

We take the security of your information seriously and implement reasonable technical, administrative, and physical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, modification, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit (e.g., using SSL/TLS) to protect your information when it is transmitted between your device and our servers;

However, no method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information, but we will take all reasonable steps to minimize the risk of unauthorized access.
